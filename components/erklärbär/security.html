Only a tip here to create a password that you can write down and wich is still safe. first of all, you have to be aware, that there are two types of hackers:

the ones, who want to do it for the fame and the bad guys, who want to steal your identity.

the bad guys are the ones, you have to focus on.

how can you do this?
choose a password like e.g. movietheatre. first step: change letters for numbers. m zero v 1 3 t h 3 4 t r 3.

second step: choose some control characters like § or & and place them inside your password. you can choose any place. e.g. m0v§13th34tr3&.

you can carry a paper with you that says movietheatre 4§end& and nobody will ever have a clou how your rules work. you can vary the rules of course. letters to numbers: 3=e, 1=i, 4=a, 5=s, 6=g, 8=b
